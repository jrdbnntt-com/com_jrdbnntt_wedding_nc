About the Venue

Getting There

The venue is located at: Salty's on Alki Beach
Address: 1936 Harbor Ave SW, Seattle, WA 98126

You will want to arrive by coming via the West Seattle Bridge or it is possible to take a water taxi from downtown Seattle.

Parking

We recommend using Uber, Lyft or Seattle Yellow Cab. You are also free to drive, there is some parking available at the venue, as well as some street parking on Alki Beach.

Remember that this will be a summer wedding and Alki beach can get a little bit crowded. If you are parking at the venue, we highly recommend carpooling.
